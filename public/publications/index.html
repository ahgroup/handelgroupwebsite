I used to list all our publications here, with the ability to filter by topic. It was nice, but it was very tedious to maintain, Hugo/Wowchemy kept breaking things all the time. So I finally gave up. Now, instead of trying to manage my publications on this website, I’m just pointing you to my (fairly well maintained) publication entries at NCBI/NIH My Bibliography or Google Scholar.

I also used to have the pdfs of all my papers here. This doesn’t work anymore with the new setup. But if for any reason there is a paper you want to get and can’t, contact me and I’d be happy to send you the pdf.

Also, there are some papers that members of our group co-authored on which I am not listed. To find those, go to that group member’s website.
